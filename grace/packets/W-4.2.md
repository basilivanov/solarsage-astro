## Decision

Implement scoring layer that processes `AstroSignal[]` and produces:
1. **day_status** — overall day quality (supportive, steady, tense)
2. **sphere_scores** — scores for life spheres (career, relationships, health, creativity)
3. **top_signals** — strongest 5 signals for the day

**Why:** Scoring transforms raw astrological data into actionable insights. Day status provides quick overview, sphere scores enable targeted guidance, top signals highlight key influences.
